About the Maths Support Scheme

We are a peer-led maths support community designed to help students build confidence and improve their mathematical skills.

What we offer

  • Drop-in support sessions
  • One-to-one peer mentoring
  • Workshops on key topics
  • Online resources and guides

Who we are for

Our service is open to all students who would like help with mathematics, regardless of level or background.
